About Me


Step into my world of digital enchantment! I'm Nithya Perera, an ambitious final year computer science student at The Open University of Sri Lanka. With a deep passion for web development and design, I am constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.

During my journey, I have immersed myself in various learning experiences to refine my skills.
